Factsheet - refusal of entry and patron bans / barring

This is a comprehensive and practical document. It includes information on options that licencees and staff have for escalating responses to deal with a troublesome patron. It highlights the need to consider laws (e.g. anti-discrimination), includes case studies, and useful information to consider when developing a venue's strategy for barring patrons. (Liquor & Gaming NSW, Feb 2016).
