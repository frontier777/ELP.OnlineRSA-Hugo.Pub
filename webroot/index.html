Who should do RSA training

Basically, you MUST have a current and valid, state-approved, Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol SITHFAB021 (RSA) certificate if your job involves the selling, serving or manufacturing of alcohol, anywhere in Australia.

  • Staff serving or supplying alcohol.
  • Licensees. (varies in some states)
  • Security staff in licensed premises or events.
  • Airline cabin crew.
  • Backpackers looking for work.
  • School leavers seeking a job.
  • Aged care and hospital staff.
  • Accommodation staff where alcohol is served.
  • Alcohol home delivery.
  • Wine tasting or cellar door sales staff.
  • Alcohol manufacturing workers.
  • Anyone who needs a RSA refresher.
